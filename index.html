THE PROCESS

I handle every job individually. My main focus is on active communication with the client - only that way we can achieve exactly what the client wants.

1

Planning

We will discuss what do you need. You can be very concrete, or not concrete at all: I can precisely follow what you said, or give you a few proposals of what we could do. You can even draw your ideas on paper and send them to me. I will set up everything needed for the project. Do you also need any graphic design work? Photo editing? Videography? No problem - I can also handle that for you.

2

Deep work

I then start designing, creating and hosting the project. All throughout this phase, I will actively communicate with you, show you progress and tweak things to your liking. Whether it is a webpage, personal portfolio, e-commerce store, web app, automation software or anything else, the solution will be custom-made for your needs.

3

Finish line

At this time, we will have a nearly finished product. Here, we will handle the final adjustments: "Could we actually do the title a bit larger?" "Can we add this section?" After your review and polishing the final little things I will publish the project. This is the finish line.
